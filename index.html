Frequently Asked Questions

How does ConnectCRM integrate with WhatsApp?

ConnectCRM uses WhatsApp's official Business API to provide seamless integration. We handle all the technical setup - you just need to connect your business number and you're ready to go. No coding required.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. ConnectCRM is month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with no penalties.

What happens if I exceed my chat limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional chat packs. We never cut off service unexpectedly.

How secure is my data?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're fully compliant with Nigerian data protection regulations and GDPR for international clients.

Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?

Yes! You can migrate your existing WhatsApp Business number to ConnectCRM without losing any chat history or contacts.
